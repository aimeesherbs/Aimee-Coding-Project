Who is Riverside Art Center

Riverside Art Center, a 501c3 organization, was founded to inspire creativity  through the arts. 
Located in beautiful & historic downtown Wapakoneta, Ohio, Riverside Art Center is home to a
variety of art classes and exhibits for all ages.
